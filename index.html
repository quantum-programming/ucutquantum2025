About the Workshop

This one‑day workshop aims to foster in‑depth discussions on practical quantum computing across algorithms, error correction/mitigation, simulation, and characterization/verification/validation.

Date & Location

  • Date: December 4, 2025 (CT)
  • Location: William Eckhardt Research Center (Room 161), University of Chicago
